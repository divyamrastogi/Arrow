Insights into Sohna Rural
Sohna Rural is an upcoming locality of Gurgaon, Haryana. Famous for its hot water springs and ancient Shiva temple, Sohna is a popular tourist location in Haryana, especially as a weekend and conference retreat. Situated in the foothills of Aravalis, the town derived its name from the gold dust which was found in the sand of streams, after heavy rains in the beds of the neighboring torrents. This area has great connectivity and prospects with the proposed 1,483 km-long DMIC, new Metro linkages, and the forthcoming KMP bypass coming up in the vicinity. The Sohna-Gurgaon Road and National Highway 71B are the roads connecting neighbouring areas of this place. Basai Dhankot Railway Station is the major station close to this area. Indira Gandhi International Airport is the airport closest to this area. Majoka Industries, Hyline Auto Industries and Ambica Industries are some of the well known industries prominent in and around this area. K.R. Mangalam University, GD Goenka University Gurgaon and Jindal College are few reputed college located close by. Alashka hospital, Upkar Hospital and Guri Hospital are few renowned hospitals nearby. Ambience Mall and Omaxe Gurgaon Mall are some of the malls close to this area offering good recreational options. The area also houses prominent schools, colleges, hospitals, shopping and other entertainment avenues. The presence of basic establishment has transformed Sohna Rural as one of the most preferred residential areas of Haryana.
